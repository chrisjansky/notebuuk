meet your new favourite notebuuk

notebuuk — a continuation of an award-winning Graduation Project — was born out of passion for honest craft, quality materials and unique design.

  • Each notebuuk is completely handmade by Christian & Tereza, two Design Master’s students from Czech Republic. From folding of the papers to final packaging, each notebuuk is unmistakeably original.

  • The choice of materials is what sets notebuuk apart. The juxtaposition of wood veneer inlay and book cloth — a subtle homage to the heydays of bookmaking — showcases what can craft and modern approach achieve.

  • Doing away with the flimsy, over-bleached papers, notebuuks are proud to use eco-certified, recycled paper stock of multiple shades for all the possible needs in the world.
